Iloilo is a home of historical landmarks, quaint beaches and isalnds, heritage homes plus it has tasty and unique cuisine. It's picturesque natural wonders and it's friendly locals will make this destination that it will feel like a second home.

Molo Church

Molo parish church is an iconic Historical site in Iloilo City. Famous being the only Gothic-Renaissance Church in the entire country outside of Manila, Molo Church attracts tourists that are eager to explore one of the historical and heritage site in Western Visayas. It is made of white coral facade that enhances the clear Gohic Renaissance elements of the structure. The Gothic features is manifested its two clear red pointed towers.

Islas De Gigantes

The Gigantes island is one of the islands in Iloilo. If you ever loved beaches like in Palawan or in Boracay. Islas de GIgantes will definitely be a good place for a vacation. The island youll see the secluded white-sand beaches, stunning rock formations and long stretch sandbars.


Molo Mansion

The Molo Mansion is also known as the Yusay-Mansion, Which is one of the most excellent historical homes in the Philippines. This has the most notable features of its high ceilings. Neoclassical, balustrades, and decorative carvings. It stands across the town plaza and at St. Anne Parish Church in Molo neighborhood. After the family moved out of the mansion.
